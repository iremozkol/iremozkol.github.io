Hello

Psychotherapy is a process wherein you explore and understand your own value judgments, beliefs, and thought patterns with the guidance of a professional.

The therapy process is rooted in collaboration with the client. It’s essential to recognize the uniqueness of each individual’s journey, needs, and capabilities, and to respond appropriately.

Through the therapeutic support I offer, you can discover solutions to both your own and others’ emotional and behavioral challenges. During this journey, you can share and delve into your personal experiences in a confidential and trusting environment, free from judgment.

It’s important to remember that everyone can struggle with challenging periods now and then; it’s a natural part of life. Never hesitate to take the first step for your well-being and seek help.

Irem Ozkol
Clinical Psychologist (B.A, MSc, MBPsS, MBACP)
